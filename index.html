Today's Specials

Vada-Pav

Vada Pav is a popular Indian street food originating from the state of Maharashtra, particularly Mumbai. Often referred to as the "Indian burger," Vada Pav consists of a spicy potato fritter (vada) sandwiched between a soft bun (pav).

Batata-Bhaji-Pav

**Batata Bhaji Pav** is a popular Indian street food featuring spiced potato curry (batata bhaji) served inside a soft pav bun.

Samosa-Pav

**Samosa Pav** is a delicious Indian street snack where a crispy, spiced potato-filled samosa is tucked inside a soft pav bun, often served with chutneys.
